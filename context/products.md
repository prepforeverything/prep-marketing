# Products

PrepEdu builds AI-powered **test-preparation** products. Each product is named **Prep +
<test>**. AI scoring and adaptive learning are the through-line across all of them.

## Product lines

| Product | What it is |
|---------|------------|
| **Prep IELTS** | IELTS prep (the four skills: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking) with AI scoring. Our flagship and largest revenue line. |
| **Prep TOEIC** | TOEIC prep for workplace/academic English certification. |
| **Prep HSK** | HSK prep for Chinese-language proficiency (relevant for TW/HK and Chinese learners). |
| **Prep PTE** | PTE Academic prep with AI scoring. |
| **Prep TOEFL** | TOEFL iBT prep with AI scoring. |
| **Prep VSTEP** | VSTEP prep — the Vietnamese Standardized Test of English Proficiency (VN-specific). |

### Supporting / PLG products

- **Dictionary** and **vocab apps** — free, product-led-growth (PLG) vehicles that bring new
  users in at low cost (owned by Learning Utilities / Growth).
- **Teacher Bee AI** — the AI learning assistant inside the products.
- **PrepTalk** — speaking-practice product (confirm exact name/spacing with the team).

## Brand-naming rules (read before naming anything externally)

- The brand is **Prep** (capitalized). Product = **Prep <Test>**, e.g. **Prep IELTS**,
  **Prep TOEIC**. Keep a space between "Prep" and the test name.
- **PREP** in ALL CAPS is for logos/marketing lockups only — not body copy.
- **PrepBee** = mascot; **Teacher Bee AI** = the AI assistant. Do not mix these up.
- Avoid **Preppy** in UI/marketing copy (informal nickname only).
- For B2B white-label deployments, the brand name is variable — never hard-code "Prep" into
  white-label assets; use `{brand_name}`.

> **Strategic note:** IELTS is > 40% of revenue and the company is deliberately
> **diversifying below 40%**. When planning a campaign, prefer opportunities that grow the
> non-IELTS lines (TOEIC, HSK, PTE, TOEFL, VSTEP). Flag campaigns that deepen IELTS
> concentration so leadership can weigh the trade-off.
