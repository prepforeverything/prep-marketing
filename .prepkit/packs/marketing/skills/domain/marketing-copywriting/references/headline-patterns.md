# Headline Patterns

## Power Word Placement Guide

| Position | Role | Example power words |
|----------|------|---------------------|
| First word | Grabs attention | Discover, Finally, Warning, Proven, New |
| Near a number | Amplifies specificity | Exactly, Instantly, In [time], Only |
| Before the benefit | Boosts desire | Guaranteed, Effortless, Remarkable, Explosive |
| Near the audience | Creates relevance | You, [Job title], [Situation word] |

---

## How-To Headlines

| Template | Worked Example |
|----------|----------------|
| How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe] | How to close your first enterprise deal in 90 days |
| How to [achieve outcome] without [pain] | How to grow your list without paid ads |
| How to [achieve outcome] even if [objection] | How to run webinars even if you hate being on camera |
| How [audience] can [outcome] | How bootstrapped founders can compete with funded rivals |
| How I [achieved outcome] in [timeframe] | How I built 10k email subscribers in 6 months |
| How to stop [negative behavior] and start [positive result] | How to stop losing leads and start closing them |

## Negative / Warning Headlines

| Template | Worked Example |
|----------|----------------|

| Template | Worked Example |
|----------|----------------|
| The [adjective] truth about [topic] | The uncomfortable truth about content marketing ROI |

## Headline Validation Checklist

- [ ] Is the primary benefit in the first 6 words?
- [ ] Does it speak to a specific audience or pain?
- [ ] Is there at least one power word?
- [ ] Is it specific enough to be credible (numbers help)?
- [ ] Would a stranger understand it in under 3 seconds?
- [ ] Does it avoid internal jargon?
- [ ] Is it under 10 words for ads, under 14 for content?
- [ ] Does it create a logical reason to keep reading?
- [ ] Does it avoid clickbait that cannot be delivered?
- [ ] Have you written at least 3 alternatives before choosing?
