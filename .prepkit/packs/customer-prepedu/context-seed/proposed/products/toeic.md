## The exam (public — confirm current rules)
- **Listening & Reading:** scored **10–990** (the score most employers quote). **Speaking & Writing:** a
  separate test, **0–200**. Workplace/academic English; score validity commonly ~2 years.
- **Used for:** employer requirements, graduation requirements, hiring/promotion thresholds — heavily
  used across Asia (incl. Vietnam corporate HR), Japan, Korea.

## Who buys it & why
- **Professional** ("Lan") needing a number for a job/promotion/graduation; **B2B** (companies upskilling
  staff). Buyer is often the employee or an L&D/HR manager.
- **Top objection:** "I just need a target score fast, around work." → efficient, mobile, targeted plan.

## AI-scoring angle (Teacher Bee AI)
- L&R is highly drillable → adaptive practice + instant scoring shows a fast, measurable climb (great for
  a "score in N weeks" narrative). S&W AI feedback differentiates vs cram-only competitors.
