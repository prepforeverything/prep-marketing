# Audience Personas — Prep IELTS

> **Status: draft / unverified.** These personas are derived from placeholder marketing
> assets and the two segments named in the kit brief. Treat them as starting hypotheses for
> messaging, not validated research. Refine via `/mkt-research` (writes proposals into
> `proposed/`). Any numbers a persona "believes" are not facts — see `claims.json`.

We market Prep IELTS to **two segments**: **Students** and **Professionals**.

---

## Persona 1 — The Student ("Minh", high-schooler / university applicant)

- **Who:** 16–22, preparing for university admission or study abroad; often guided (and
  funded) by parents. Mobile-first, social-media native.
- **Goal:** reach the band an application or scholarship requires (commonly 6.5–7.5) by a
  hard deadline.

**Pains**
- Doesn't know if they're on track; no fast feedback on Writing/Speaking.
- Deadline anxiety — the application cycle won't wait.
- Cost matters; a parent is comparing options.

**Objections**
- "Is online as good as a center like VIETOP / IELTS Fighter / DOL?"
- "Can an app really fix my Writing and Speaking?"
- "What if I pay and still don't get the band?" → answered by the guarantee (CLM-001).

**Hooks (Vietnamese-first; numbers are placeholders → unverified)**
- "Lộ trình IELTS rõ ràng để kịp hạn nộp hồ sơ — cam kết đầu ra."
  *(A clear IELTS roadmap to meet your application deadline — outcome guaranteed.)*
- "AI chấm Writing & Speaking ngay, biết mình đang ở band nào."
  *(AI scores your Writing & Speaking instantly — know your band now.)*

**CTA preference:** low-commitment, mobile — **free trial / free level test / Zalo chat**.
Avoid asking for payment up front.

---

## Persona 2 — The Professional ("Lan", working adult)

- **Who:** 24–40, working full-time; needs IELTS for a job, promotion, immigration, or a
  postgrad application. Time-poor, less price-sensitive than students.
- **Goal:** hit a specific minimum band efficiently, without disrupting work.

**Pains**
- No time for fixed-schedule classes across town.
- Rusty English; needs an efficient, targeted plan, not a generic course.
- Wants certainty that the investment pays off.

**Objections**
- "I can't commit to a class schedule." → answered by self-paced + mobile.
- "Will this actually be efficient, or will I waste evenings?"
- "Is the guarantee real, and what are the conditions?" → CLM-001.

**Hooks (Vietnamese-first; numbers are placeholders → unverified)**
- "Luyện IELTS linh hoạt quanh lịch làm việc — học mọi lúc trên điện thoại."
  *(Flexible IELTS practice around your work schedule — study anytime on mobile.)*
- "AI phản hồi tức thì giúp bạn tiến bộ nhanh, không tốn buổi tối vô ích."
  *(Instant AI feedback so you improve fast — no wasted evenings.)*

**CTA preference:** efficiency-led — **book a consult / free level test / start free trial**,
with flexible payment options surfaced (price sensitivity is lower but flexibility matters).

## Using these personas

- Lead with the **pain**, then the **mechanism** (Study Plan + AI scoring), then the
  **guarantee** — only if CLM-001 is `approved`.
- Always write **Vietnamese-first** for VN; localize per `markets/<market>.md` elsewhere.
- Match channel to persona CTA preference (see `markets/vietnam.md`: Zalo OA, hotline, Facebook).
