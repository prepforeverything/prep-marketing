# Vietnam — Locale & Market Policy

> Primary market. This file tells every agent how to speak to and sell in Vietnam.

## Language & tone
- **Vietnamese-first** for all customer-facing copy. Keep English only for natural terms
  (IELTS, band, 1-1, online).
- Tone: confident-but-honest mentor — see [`brand-voice.md`](../brand-voice.md). No hype, no
  absolute claims, admit trade-offs ("không phải con đường ngắn nhất — là con đường chắc nhất").

## Audience
- Price-sensitive, mobile-first. Two segments — Students and Professionals — see
  [`audience-personas.md`](../audience-personas.md).

## Exam cycle & intent windows
> Demand-seasonality guide for planning calendars — see [`../exam-calendar.md`](../exam-calendar.md)
> for the cross-market principle (plan **backwards** from the learner's deadline by course length).
> The window *patterns* are stable; **exact dates shift yearly and are unverified — confirm each cycle
> via `/mkt-research`** before any dated claim reaches copy. These windows drive *when* to run
> acquisition, not *what* to claim.

## Claims & regulatory
- Band-guarantee messaging must **always** pair the promise (`[[CLM-001]]`) with the
  eligibility terms (`[[CLM-002]]`).
- **Advertising law — VN Law 75/2025/QH15 (in force 2026-01-01), binding:**
  - Any outcome, comparative, or price claim needs an **approved** `claim_id` with non-empty
    evidence before it may publish (the claims gate enforces this).
  - **Comparative advertising is permitted** when backed by lawful, dated documentation — a named
    comparison is allowed **only** behind a dated source + an approved comparative `claim_id`
    (see [`../competitors.md`](../competitors.md)). Compare on verifiable facts; never disparage.
  - **KOL / KOC / affiliate** content: the creator is a legally liable advertising carrier — require
    a visible sponsored disclosure, only substantiable claims, and genuine prior use of the product.
  - Disclaimers (e.g. the CLM-002 eligibility terms) must appear at **prominence matching** the
    main claim, not buried.
- Every external number (success %, band gain, learner count) must map to an **approved**
  `claim_id` — see [`../claims.md`](../claims.md).
