## The exam (public — confirm current rules)
- **VSTEP** = Vietnamese Standardized Test of English Proficiency, aligned to a **6-level CEFR framework**
  (commonly **VSTEP 3–5 ≈ B1–C1**), four skills. Administered by **MoET-authorized universities** in Vietnam.
- **Used for (domestic VN):** university **graduation** English requirements, **civil-service** standards,
  teacher/職 standards, some postgrad admissions. **Not** an international study-abroad test.

## Who buys it & why
