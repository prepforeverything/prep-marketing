## Language & tone
- Primary language: **Traditional Chinese (zh-TW)** for all customer-facing copy. Keep English for
  test terms (IELTS, TOEFL, band).
- Tone: polite, precise, credibility-led; Taiwanese learners value detail and proof.

## Audience
- Study-abroad learners (IELTS/TOEFL) and professionals. Segment detail pending research.
