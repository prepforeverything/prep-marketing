## The exam (public — confirm current rules)
- **TOEFL iBT:** total **0–120** (Reading, Listening, Speaking, Writing — each **0–30**), **internet-based**.
  Results commonly ~4–8 days; score validity ~2 years.
- **Used for:** university admission, especially **US** (and widely accepted elsewhere); some scholarships/visas.

## Who buys it & why
- Study-abroad candidates targeting **US** universities/grad programs (where TOEFL is traditionally strong);
  Students and Professionals (postgrad). Often choosing between TOEFL and IELTS by destination preference.
- **Top objection:** "Should I take TOEFL or IELTS?" → answer by destination + the candidate's strengths;
  don't push the higher-margin line over the candidate's best fit (trust > short-term mix).

## AI-scoring angle (Teacher Bee AI)
- Independent + integrated Writing/Speaking tasks are hard to self-grade — instant AI feedback on these is
  the differentiator. Show section-level diagnostics mapped to the 0–30 bands.
