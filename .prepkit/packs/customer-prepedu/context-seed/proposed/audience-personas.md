# PROPOSAL — add Persona 3: The Parent (decision-maker / payer)

> **This is a proposal to ADD to `context/audience-personas.md`** (which today covers only Student and
> Professional). The canonical file itself notes the Student is "often guided and **funded** by
> parents" — yet the parent, who frequently controls the purchase, has no persona. Review, refine via
> `/mkt-research`, then merge as Persona 3. All numbers below are placeholders → unverified.

## Persona 3 — The Parent ("Chị Hương", parent of a high-schooler)

- **Who:** 38–52, parent of a 15–18-year-old; the **economic buyer** for the Student segment.
  Researches on Facebook/Zalo and via word-of-mouth in parent groups; the student is the end-user but
  the parent shortlists, compares, and pays.
- **Goal:** their child reaches the band a university / scholarship / study-abroad path requires —
  with proof the money is well spent and the child won't be left behind.

**Pains**
- Can't judge English quality themselves — needs **trustworthy proof** (outcomes, accreditation, real
  student results), not marketing hype.
- Fears wasting money on a course the child won't finish or that won't move the band.
- Time-pressured by the admission cycle; wants a clear plan and accountability.

**Objections**
- "How do I know this works better than a center (VIETOP / IELTS Fighter / DOL)?"
- "Will my child actually study, or just open the app once?" → answered by progress visibility + roadmap.
- "What if we pay and the band doesn't improve?" → answered by the guarantee ({{CLM-001}}) **with its
  eligibility terms ({{CLM-002}})** — parents read the conditions.

**Hooks (Vietnamese-first; numbers are placeholders → unverified)**
- "Theo dõi tiến bộ của con mỗi tuần — minh bạch, có lộ trình rõ ràng."
  *(See your child's progress every week — transparent, with a clear roadmap.)*
- "Cam kết đầu ra kèm điều kiện rõ ràng — đầu tư yên tâm cho kỳ thi của con."
  *(An outcome guarantee with clear conditions — invest with confidence for your child's exam.)*

**CTA preference:** trust-led — **free consultation / level test for the child / talk to an adviser on
Zalo or hotline**. Surface proof (results, guarantee terms, accreditation) prominently; flexible payment
matters. Decision is often joint — give the parent shareable proof to discuss with the child.

## Cross-persona note for merge
- The Student remains the **user**; the Parent is the **buyer** for under-18s. Campaigns targeting
  high-schoolers should carry a parent-facing proof layer (results + guarantee terms) alongside the
  student-facing motivation.
- Seasonality: the Parent acts hardest in the **admission-crunch** and **back-to-school** windows
  (see `context/markets/vietnam.md` → Exam cycle & intent windows).
