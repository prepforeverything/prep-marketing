## The exam (public — confirm current rules)
- **PTE Academic:** **10–90** scale (Global Scale of English), fully **computer-based**, **machine/AI-scored**.
  Results typically fast (often ~2 days). Integrated-skills tasks (one task scores multiple skills).
- **Used for:** study and visa (notably **Australia / New Zealand / UK**) — accepted for student & migration visas.

## Who buys it & why
- Study-abroad and migration candidates (esp. AUS/NZ-bound) who want a **fast, objective** result and find
  IELTS Speaking (human examiner) stressful. Professional + Student both apply.
- **Top objection:** "Is PTE accepted where I'm going?" → confirm destination acceptance up front; "Will the
  machine score me fairly?" → PTE is designed for machine scoring; our AI practice mirrors that.
