## Method (how these three segments were derived)

These are **needs-based** segments (organized by the job the learner is hiring PrepEdu to do and the
intent window driving it), not demographic buckets. Each is anchored to a Stage-00 *structural*
signal class and to Stage-01 sourced market signals (S1–S5) + the consumer-voice phrase bank
(P/D/O codes refer to `market-research.md` §4).

| Seg | Needs-based name | Stage-00 structural analogue | Stage-00 read (mapped, not copied) | Built on real audience |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A** | Deadline-driven admissions student | `prospecting-core` → **scale-room HIGH** | Fresh cold pain-hook audience with headroom (low frequency) — scale here before adding spend to saturated lines | Student "Minh" (`context/audience-personas.md`) |
| **B** | Time-poor working certificate-seeker | `career-switchers` + `prospecting-fresh` → **scale-room HIGH** | Highest-headroom cold-prospecting analogue; treat its thin high-CTR tests as hypotheses, not proven winners | Professional "Lan" (`context/audience-personas.md`) |

> **Fatigue caution carried from Stage 00 (structural):** the broad-reach / lookalike line is the
> `PAUSE`-fatigued, low-scale-room analogue (high frequency, declining CTR). Mapped to VN: do **not**
> pour budget into a saturated broad lookalike of the whole learner base (S4 says reach is
> effectively unlimited, so the failure mode is frequency, not reach). Win by rotating fresh creative
> into the high-scale-room cold segments A/B and opening whitespace C.

## Segment A — Deadline-driven admissions student

*Needs-based job: "Get me to the band my university/scholarship application requires, before the
admission deadline I cannot move."*

- **Demographics:** 16–22, high-schooler or university applicant in VN; mobile-first, social-media
  native; commonly funded/shortlisted by a parent (the economic buyer — see persona proposal
  `context/proposed/audience-personas.md` → "Chị Hương"). `Source · context/audience-personas.md (Persona 1) · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Psychographics:** goal-anchored and time-anxious; wants a concrete target band (6.5–7.5) and a
  visible plan; trusts proof + peer scale over hype; reads guarantee *conditions*, not just the
  promise. `Source · context/positioning.md §"The two segments" · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Pains (from phrase bank):**
  - P3 "Sắp tới hạn nộp hồ sơ rồi mà band chưa đủ, lo quá." (deadline + fear) — **High** hook.
  - P1 "Học mãi mà Speaking vẫn 5.5, không lên nổi." (stuck-band wall) — matches S2 (Speaking 5.7 is
    VN's weakest skill; modal ~6.0). **High**.
  - P2 "Writing toàn bị trừ điểm mà chẳng biết sai chỗ nào." (no feedback loop) — **High**.
  `Source · market-research.md §4A (P1–P3) + S2 · as-of 2024 / 2026-06-08`
- **Buy-triggers:** a hard admission/scholarship deadline inside the **Admission-crunch (Jun–Sep)**
  or **Year-end/study-abroad (Nov–Jan)** intent window; a band-check result that shows the gap;
  parent green-light on proof. `Source · context/markets/vietnam.md §"Exam cycle & intent windows" (patterns stable; exact dates unverified — confirm each cycle) · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Stats that frame the segment:**
  - VN modal Academic IELTS is **6.0 (~21% of takers)**; only ~5% reach 8.0+ → the gap to a 6.5–7.5
    admissions band is the core job. `Source · VnExpress International / VietnamNet, IELTS partners' 2023–2024 data · as-of 2024`
  - The IELTS→domestic-admission advantage is **narrowing** (draft MoET regulation 2026-01-28 caps
    cert bonus at 1.5) → urgency is shifting from "IELTS = ticket in" toward learners who still need
    a *real* band. *(Draft — treat direction as strong signal, not enacted law; see Q7.)*
    `Source · VnExpress International, "Students dismayed as IELTS advantage shrinks…" · as-of 2026-03-01`
- **Product-fit:** **Prep IELTS** flagship — personalized Study Plan (answers D2 "lộ trình rõ ràng")
  + AI scoring on Writing/Speaking (answers P1/P2, the sourced S2 wedge). Guarantee
  ({{CLM-001}} + terms {{CLM-002}}) only once approved — **unverified — pending approval**; never
  lead with it. Anchor trust with `[[CLM-008]]`. `Source · context/positioning.md + context/products.md · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Stage-00 action (structural):** scale-room **HIGH** — this is a fresh cold-prospecting analogue
  with headroom; test P1/P2/P3 pain-hook creative into cold before re-spending on any saturated
  audience.

## Segment B — Time-poor working certificate-seeker

*Needs-based job: "Get me a specific minimum band for work/promotion/immigration/postgrad —
efficiently, without disrupting my job."*

- **Demographics:** 24–40, working full-time in VN; needs a certificate for a job, promotion,
  immigration, or a postgrad application; time-poor, less price-sensitive than students.
  `Source · context/audience-personas.md (Persona 2) · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Psychographics:** efficiency-maximizing and schedule-constrained; rusty English, wants a
  targeted plan not a generic course; wants certainty the investment pays off; evaluates on
  time-ROI. `Source · context/positioning.md §"Professionals" · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Pains (from phrase bank):**
  - P4 "Đi làm cả ngày, tối về mệt, lấy đâu thời gian học IELTS." (time-poverty) — **High**.
  - P5 "Mất gốc tiếng Anh mấy năm rồi, giờ học lại từ đâu?" (re-starter) — **Med** (better as a
    targeted segment hook than a cold mass hook).
  - D4 "Cần chứng chỉ tiếng Anh để thăng tiến / nộp công ty." (career driver) — **Med**.
  `Source · market-research.md §4 (P4, P5, D4) · as-of 2026-06-08`
- **Buy-triggers:** an employer/immigration/postgrad deadline; a promotion or job-change moment; the
  realization that fixed-schedule offline classes "across town" are unworkable (sets up self-paced +
  mobile). `Source · context/audience-personas.md (Persona 2 pains/objections) · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Stats that frame the segment:**
  - VN is a saturated **mobile-social** market — **76.2M** active social identities (~75% of
    population); Facebook ad reach 76.2M, TikTok 18+ 40.9M → this segment is reachable at scale on
    mobile, so the constraint is creative differentiation + frequency, not reach.
    `Source · DataReportal "Digital 2025: Vietnam" · as-of 2025-03-03`
  - Company strategy diversifies **below 40% IELTS** → this segment is the natural lane to probe
    **Prep TOEIC / VSTEP** (workplace-cert demand), de-risking the S3 IELTS policy headwind.
    `Source · context/products.md §"Strategic note" · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Product-fit:** flexible, self-paced, mobile **Prep IELTS** — *and* the diversification wedge
  into **Prep TOEIC / Prep VSTEP** for workplace certification (new-product copy needs its own
  approved claims before any outcome promise). AI feedback answers "will I waste evenings?" (instant
  scoring → efficient). Trust anchor `[[CLM-008]]`. `Source · context/products.md · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Stage-00 action (structural):** scale-room **HIGH** (`career-switchers` analogue is the
  single highest-headroom cold audience) — but treat its thin, short-runtime high-CTR tests as
  **hypotheses to re-run at scale**, not proven winners (Stage-00 `LOW_CONFIDENCE` flag).

## Segment C — Study-abroad "real-band" seeker (whitespace)

*Needs-based job: "I need a real, defensible band for study-abroad / scholarship / visa — not a
domestic bonus point that regulation is eroding."*

- **Demographics:** 17–30, VN students and young professionals applying to overseas universities,
  scholarships, or visa pathways; overlaps the upper edge of Segment A and the younger edge of
  Segment B; high-intent, higher willingness-to-invest. *(Derived from S3; not yet a canonical
  persona — flagged for validation.)* `Source · market-research.md §5.3 (whitespace angle) · as-of 2026-06-08`
- **Psychographics:** outcome-serious and credential-aware; explicitly wants "band thật" (a real
  band) over domestic shortcuts; less swayed by price, more by credibility and a track to a hard
  external requirement. `Source · market-research.md §4B (D3) · as-of 2026-06-08`
- **Pains / desires (from phrase bank):**
  - D3 "Học để đi du học, cần band thật chứ không phải điểm cộng." — **High**; the exact emerging
    wedge (rides S3).
  - D1 "Mình cần 6.5 để đủ điều kiện xét tuyển / học bổng." — **High** (concrete target + stake).
  - P1/P2 still apply (Writing/Speaking are the bands that gate a *real* score).
  `Source · market-research.md §4 (D1, D3, P1–P2) · as-of 2026-06-08`
- **Buy-triggers:** a fall-intake application or scholarship/visa deadline (the
  **Year-end/study-abroad Nov–Jan** window); the realization post-policy that a domestic bonus no
  longer substitutes for a real band. `Source · context/markets/vietnam.md §"Exam cycle & intent windows" · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Stats that frame the segment:**
  - The draft MoET regulation (2026-01-28) caps cert bonus at **1.5** (down from up to 3) and forces
    convert-*or*-bonus (not both) → demand shifts toward learners who need a **real** band for
    study-abroad/scholarship/visa — demand the domestic-bonus erosion does **not** touch. *(Draft;
    direction is a strong signal, see Q7.)* `Source · VnExpress International · as-of 2026-03-01`
  - VN ranks **29th of ~40** on the Academic IELTS league table and partners publish **no** country
    headcount → size this by intent windows, never by a headline test-taker number (Q1 is
    unresolvable by design). `Source · IELTS partners' 2023–2024 data via VnExpress International · as-of 2024`
- **Product-fit:** **Prep IELTS** for the band itself, positioned on "band thật cho du học" — the
  personalized roadmap + AI scoring as the credible mechanism to reach a defensible external score.
  Trust anchor `[[CLM-008]]`; no outcome/band-gain promise until {{CLM-006}}/{{CLM-007}} are
  approved (**unverified — pending approval**). `Source · context/positioning.md · as-of 2026-06-06`
- **Stage-00 action (structural):** **WHITESPACE** — the zero-spend (`lapsed-users` analogue)
  segment to open deliberately. High strategic value, untested; start small, measure on the
  attention proxy (hook-rate/CTR) before scaling.

## Segment selection rationale (for the brief stage)

1. **A + B are the scale plays** — both map to Stage-00 high-scale-room cold-prospecting; they are
   where fresh creative has headroom now.
2. **C is the strategic whitespace** — policy-driven (S3), aligns with the company's "diversify the
   demand thesis" and the study-abroad real-band wedge; lower volume, higher intent, untested.
3. **Avoid the saturated broad lookalike** — the Stage-00 `PAUSE`/fatigued analogue; reach is not the
   constraint in VN (S4), frequency is.
4. **Every outcome promise stays gated** — only `[[CLM-008]]` is asserted; all band-gain / success-%
   / pricing / guarantee references are `{{CLM-xxx}}` placeholders, **unverified — pending
   approval**, until the claims gate clears them for VN.
