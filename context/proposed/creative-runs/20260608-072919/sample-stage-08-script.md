## SCRIPT — "6 tuần" (≤15s, 9:16, muted-first)

> Beats are timed to sum to ≤15s. Each beat names the **shot**, the **on-screen VN text** (burned in,
> muted-first), the **VO** (Vietnamese, with an internal English gloss in *italics* — the gloss never
> ships), the **sound/motion**, and the **force/motivator it serves**. This beat structure is what the
> Phase-2 Stage-08 *schema* will formalize into typed fields.

**Beat 1 — HOOK · 0.0–2.0s** · *serves: PUSH (A1 deadline + A2 stuck skill)*
- **Shot:** Tight on a phone screen showing a mock band card "Speaking 5.5"; a calendar in the corner ticking "6 tuần".
- **On-screen text:** `Còn 6 tuần nộp hồ sơ — Speaking vẫn 5.5?`
- **VO:** "Còn sáu tuần nữa là tới hạn… mà Speaking vẫn 5.5." *(Six weeks to the deadline… and Speaking is still 5.5.)*
- **Sound/motion:** Clock tick SFX; subtle red pulse on "6 tuần". Snap cut in.
- **Note:** "5.5 / 6.5" are the *learner's own* current/target band, **not** a PrepEdu outcome claim (see ledger).

**Beat 2 — AGITATE · 2.0–4.0s** · *serves: PUSH (A2 the wall)*
- **Shot:** Quick montage — crossed-out notebook pages, a learner sighing at a laptop.
- **On-screen text:** `Học mãi mà không biết sai ở đâu.`
- **VO:** "Học mãi mà chẳng biết mình sai chỗ nào, nên mãi không lên." *(You study and study but never know what you're getting wrong — so you never improve.)*
- **Sound/motion:** Music drops to a single anxious note. Fast jump cuts.

**Beat 3 — MECHANISM · 4.0–6.5s** · *serves: PULL (A4) + answers O2 "can an app fix Speaking/Writing?"*
- **Shot:** Screen-capture of AI feedback marking a Writing sentence — error highlighted, fix shown; a Speaking waveform with a pronunciation flag.
- **On-screen text:** `AI chấm Writing & Speaking — chỉ ra đúng lỗi cần sửa.`
- **VO:** "Prep chấm điểm Writing và Speaking bằng AI — chỉ cho bạn đúng chỗ sai cần sửa." *(Prep scores your Writing and Speaking with AI — showing you exactly what to fix.)*
- **Sound/motion:** Music lifts (resolution). UI highlight animation.

**Beat 4 — ROADMAP · 6.5–9.0s** · *serves: PULL (A4 roadmap to a named band)*
- **Shot:** A clean personalized Study Plan UI — today's tasks, a progress line climbing toward a "6.5" flag.
- **On-screen text:** `Lộ trình rõ ràng — biết mỗi ngày học gì.`
- **VO:** "Một lộ trình rõ ràng tới band bạn cần — mỗi ngày biết chính xác phải học gì." *(A clear roadmap to the band you need — every day you know exactly what to study.)*
- **Sound/motion:** Upward motion; checkmarks tick on.

**Beat 5 — PROOF · 9.0–11.5s** · *serves: DEFUSE anxiety (A5 social proof)* — **claims-bearing beat**
- **Shot:** Logo lockup + a counter animating to 500.000+; faces/avatars grid.
- **On-screen text:** `Hơn 500.000 học viên đã tin chọn Prep [[CLM-008]]`
- **VO:** "Hơn năm trăm nghìn học viên đã tin chọn Prep." *(Over five hundred thousand learners have chosen Prep.)*
- **Sound/motion:** Confident swell. *(This is the **only** asserted PrepEdu number — full anchor text + term "học viên" on the line, so **this shipping line** is publish-clean.)*

**Beat 6 — CTA · 11.5–13.5s** · *serves: DEFUSE habit (low-commitment trigger)*
- **Shot:** A "Kiểm tra band miễn phí" button; a 30-second mock band-check flow.
- **On-screen text:** `Kiểm tra band miễn phí — biết ngay còn thiếu bao nhiêu.`
- **VO:** "Kiểm tra trình độ miễn phí — biết ngay bạn còn cách band mục tiêu bao xa." *(Free level check — know instantly how far you are from your target band.)*
- **Sound/motion:** Button tap SFX; cursor taps.

**Beat 7 — END CARD · 13.5–15.0s** · *brand + CTA repeat*
- **Shot:** Prep logo, Zalo OA badge, the CTA button held.
- **On-screen text:** `Prep — Nhắn Zalo OA để nhận lộ trình.`
- **VO:** *(none — let the end-card hold; muted-safe.)*
- **Sound/motion:** Logo sting.

**Total runtime: 15.0s.** Muted-first: every spoken line is also burned-in VN text. No claim except CLM-008.
