## Language & tone
- Primary languages: **English + Traditional Chinese (zh-HK)**. Consider **Cantonese** phrasing for
  video/social. Bilingual copy is normal; lead with the language of the placement.
- Tone: efficient, credible, internationally-minded.

## Audience
- University-admission and immigration IELTS learners; high English proficiency. Segment detail
  pending research.
