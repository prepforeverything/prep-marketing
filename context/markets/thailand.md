# Thailand — Locale & Market Policy

> **Draft locale policy.** Language, channels, and regulatory rules below are general facts and
> safe to follow. PrepEdu-specific audience, pricing, and competitor detail are **pending** — run

## Language & tone
- Primary language: **Thai (th)** for all customer-facing copy. Keep English only for test terms
  (IELTS, TOEIC, band).
- Tone: polite and warm; Thai register is hierarchy- and courtesy-aware. Avoid blunt imperatives.

## Audience
- Study-abroad and career English learners (IELTS/TOEIC). Segment detail pending research.

  consent; honor opt-out; document data handling. Treat Zalo-equivalent (LINE) messaging as
  consent-gated.
- Every external number maps to an **approved** `claim_id`; per-market claims must be re-verified
  for Thailand, not copied from VN.
