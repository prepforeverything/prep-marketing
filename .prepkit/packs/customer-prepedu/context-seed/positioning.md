## Positioning narrative

Prep IELTS is the **AI-powered IELTS course that gets you to your target band** — combining a
structured Study Plan with **AI scoring** (instant feedback on Writing and Speaking) and the
guidance of a dedicated teacher. We pair the convenience and price of self-study with the
accountability of a real outcome promise.

## The two segments

We position to two distinct audiences. Full personas live in `audience-personas.md`.

### 1. Students
- **Goal:** the band needed for university admission or study abroad (often a high target,
  e.g. 6.5–7.5).
- **Driver:** deadline pressure (application cycles), price sensitivity, parental influence.
- **Angle:** structured plan + guarantee = a safe path to the score the application needs.

### 2. Professionals
- **Goal:** a certificate for work, immigration, or career advancement — often a specific
  minimum band, fit around a busy schedule.
- **Driver:** limited time, wants efficiency and flexibility, less price-sensitive than
  students.
- **Angle:** flexible, self-paced, AI feedback any time = results without quitting your job.
