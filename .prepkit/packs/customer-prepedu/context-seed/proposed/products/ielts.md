## The exam (public — confirm current rules)
- **Scoring:** 9-band scale (0–9, half-band steps), reported per skill + overall.
- **Skills:** Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking. **Academic** (study) vs **General Training** (work/migration).
- **Delivery:** computer-delivered (results ~3–5 days) or paper (~13 days). **One Skill Retake** lets a
  candidate re-sit a single skill. No cap on attempts (fee per sitting). Score valid ~2 years.
- **Used for:** university admission, scholarships, study-abroad, skilled migration (globally accepted).

## Who buys it & why
- **Student** (admission/scholarship deadline → needs a target band) and **Professional** (job/PR/postgrad).
  See `context/audience-personas.md`; for under-18s the **Parent** is the buyer (see `proposed/audience-personas.md`).
- **Top objection:** "Will an app fix my Writing/Speaking?" and "What if I don't hit the band?" → AI
  scoring + the outcome guarantee with eligibility terms.

## AI-scoring angle (Teacher Bee AI)
- Writing & Speaking are the hardest to self-assess — instant AI band feedback is the core differentiator.
  Lead with "know your band now, see what to fix," not generic course content.
