# Ad Copy Templates

Platform-specific copy templates, character limits, and formulas for paid advertising.

---

## Google Search — Responsive Search Ads (RSA)

**Specs:** 15 headlines (max 30 chars each), 4 descriptions (max 90 chars each). Google auto-tests combinations.

### Headline Formulas

| # | Formula | Example |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Keyword + Benefit | "Project Management — Save 5h/Week" |
| 2 | Problem + Solution | "Missed Deadlines? Fix Them Today" |
| 3 | Social Proof | "Trusted by 10,000+ Teams" |
| 4 | CTA + Urgency | "Start Free — No Credit Card" |
| 5 | Differentiator | "All-in-One. No Hidden Fees." |

### Description Formulas

| # | Formula | Example |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Problem → Solution → Proof | "Chasing deadlines costs time. [Product] automates task tracking — 94% of teams hit sprints on time." |
| 2 | Feature → Benefit → CTA | "Real-time dashboards. See bottlenecks before they block delivery. Start your free trial." |
| 3 | Social Proof → Specificity | "Over 10,000 teams cut onboarding time by 60%. Join them — free 14-day trial, no card needed." |

### Pin Strategy

- Pin headline 1: primary keyword or brand name
- Pin headline 2: core benefit or differentiator
- Leave remaining headlines unpinned — let Google optimize
- Never pin all 15; unpinned headlines enable algorithm learning

## Meta / Facebook

**Specs:**
- Primary text: 125 chars visible before "See more" (write the hook in the first 125)
- Headline: 40 chars (feed) / 25 chars (stories)
- Description: 30 chars (optional, shown in link previews)

### Primary Text Structure (Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA)

```
[Pain point hook — 1 sentence, 125 chars max]

[Solution statement — 1-2 sentences]

[Proof: specific number, testimonial, or result]

[CTA — low friction for TOFU, direct for BOFU]
```

### 3-Line Hook Patterns (first 3 lines before fold)

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Question hook | "Still running campaigns with no clear ROAS target?" |
| Stat hook | "70% of ad spend is wasted on wrong audiences." |
| Bold claim | "We cut CAC by 40% in 30 days. Here's the exact setup." |
| Empathy hook | "Running ads that don't convert is exhausting. You're not alone." |

### UGC-Style Patterns

- First-person voice: "I was spending $5k/month on ads with zero results until..."
- Conversational, no hard sell in first 2 lines
- Native format: avoid stock images, overly polished graphics
- Show the product in context, not in isolation

## LinkedIn

**Specs:**
- Intro text: 600 chars total; first ~150 chars visible before "See more" — lead with the hook
- Headline: 70 chars
- Image: 1200×627 px (single image), 1080×1080 px (square)

### Intro Hook Formulas (150 chars)

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Stat + audience | "86% of B2B buyers research vendors before replying to outreach. Here's how to be findable." |
| Problem + consequence | "Your sales team closes 1 in 20 demos. The gap is usually in the ICP definition." |
| Contrarian take | "Most companies focus on more leads. The highest-performing teams focus on fewer, better leads." |

### B2B Body Structure (600 chars)

```
[Hook — 150 chars]

[Problem context — 1-2 sentences]

[Solution + specific outcome — 1-2 sentences]

[Social proof or data point]

[CTA — download, book a call, learn more]
```

**Tone notes:** Professional but direct. Avoid jargon. Active voice. Specific claims outperform vague authority ("we help teams" becomes "teams using [product] close 35% more pipeline").

## TikTok

**Specs:**
- In-Feed: 9:16 vertical video, 5-60 seconds, primary text up to 100 chars
- Hook must land in first 3 seconds — thumb-stops are earned, not assumed
- Native lo-fi formats outperform polished production in most verticals

### Video Script Structure

| Segment | Duration | Goal |
|---------|----------|------|
| Hook | 0-3s | Stop the scroll — bold statement, question, or visual surprise |
| Problem | 3-15s | Establish pain point the audience recognizes |
| Solution | 15-45s | Show the product solving the problem (demo, transformation, result) |
| CTA | 45-60s | One clear action: "Link in bio", "Download now", "Comment X for the guide" |

### Native / Lo-Fi Patterns

- Talking-head to camera: personal, authentic, high trust
- Screen recording with voiceover: works for SaaS, tools, tutorials
- Before/after split: visual proof of transformation
- Duet or stitch: commentary on trending content in your niche

## Copy Best Practices

| Practice | Guidance |
|----------|----------|
| Benefit-led | Lead with what the user gets, not what the product does |
| Specific numbers | "Saves 4 hours/week" converts better than "saves time" |
| Social proof | Include a specific claim: customers, results, years of experience |
| Message match | Ad copy must match the landing page headline — mismatches kill CVR |
| Test 3-5 variations | Never launch with a single creative; need a control and variants |
| Front-load keywords | On Google Search, the target keyword in headline 1 improves CTR and Quality Score |
| Channel adaptation | Copy that works on Meta does not transfer directly to LinkedIn or TikTok — adapt tone, length, and format |
